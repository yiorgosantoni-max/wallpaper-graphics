    Before you commission

    What does a commission cost?

    A scene on its own is $1. A scene with one person is $2. A scene with two or more people is $3. Revisions are unlimited and free at every tier — you keep asking for changes until the plate is what you pictured.

    How does the USDT payment work?

    Send 1, 2 or 3 USDT — whichever matches your format — on the TRC20 network to TKX1h9L4wWjimF8UVWHQLtFYvWv7TTHdnB. Paste the transaction hash into the commission form so the payment can be matched to your brief. Use the TRC20 network only — funds sent on another network cannot be recovered.

    What photographs do I send?

    One well-lit, front-facing photograph per person, at the highest resolution you have. Attach them to the email the form opens.

    How long does it take?

    The first draft usually arrives within a day of the payment confirming. Revisions come back faster.

    What do I receive?

    A high-resolution file sized for wherever you said it would hang, plus a matching crop for a second screen if you want one.
